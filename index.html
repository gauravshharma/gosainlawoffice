Legal Practice Areas

Criminal Law

Cheating, Fraud, Assault, Murder, Bail, Dowry Demand, Dowry Death, Narcotics, Cheque bounce case, Defamation, Maintenance, Cruelty.

Civil Law

Recovery of money, Injunction against property, Nuisance, Compensation against damages.

Property Law

Landlord-Tenant Disputes, Ancestral Property matters, Land agreements, Transfer of property, Partition of property, Drafting of Lease or Sale Deeds, Will, Due Diligence of Property.

Family/Matrimonial

Family Matters, Restitution, Divorce Cases, Dowry Cases, Maintenance Cases, Domestic Violence, Child Custody, Adoption.

Contracts Drafting

Insurance/Claims

Motor Accident Claims, Insurance Claims, Consumer Protection Act, Claims against Builders.
